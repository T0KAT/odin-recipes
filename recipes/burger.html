Burger Recipe

The Father of all Burgers:

Todays Recipe is the "Father of all Burgers".

If you follow this recipe you are gonna know about the foundational steps to take to create your own custom burger.

Ingredients:

  • 1x Burger patty
  • 1x Slice of Cheese
  • 1x Salad-Leaves
  • 1x Tomato
  • 1x Burger-Bread
  • 1x Onion
  • 1x Sauce of your choice
  1. Slice the Burger-Bread in half
  2. Put the Sauce of your choice on top and bottom bun
  3. Now put the rest of the ingredeients in the following order on-top of the bottom bun: Burger patty, Salad-Leaves, Tomato, Onion, Slice of Cheese
  4. Lastly put the top bun on top to finish the burger
