Pasta Recipe

Penne Chicken-Spinach

Today we have a recipe for all people that are bored of plain pasta.

Try this out if you want to eat nice variation that is not too alienating.

Ingredients

  • 500g Penne
  • 300g Chicken
  • 100g Spinach
  • 50g Parmesan
  1. Start out by boiling the Penne till they are medium cooked
  2. While the Penne are almost finished start cooking the chicken soon followed after by the Spinach
  3. When the chicken starts to get a golden-brown-color mix the penne with the chicken and spinach
  4. After serving add Parmisan to your liking

ENJOY!!
